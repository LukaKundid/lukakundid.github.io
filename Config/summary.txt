Luka Kundid, born on June 2nd, 2001, in Zagreb, is focused on developing a
career in the field of multimedia computing. He completed his primary
education at Velika Mlaka Elementary School and continued his studies at
Velika Gorica Gymnasium, where he attended the science and mathematics
program. He graduated in Multimedia Computing from Algebra University.

Luka stands out in 3D modeling, using software such as Blender and
Cinema4D. He also possesses skills in UI and UX design. He is experienced in
video editing and post-production, working with tools such as Adobe Premiere,
Adobe After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve.

In 2019, he obtained the "Certificate in Advanced English" at the C1 level,
awarded by Cambridge University Press & Assessment English.
His education, certifications, and practical experience make him a competent
contributor to team projects. Always eager to learn, Luka is ready for new
challenges and continuous improvement.
